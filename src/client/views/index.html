Instructions

Enter the address for an online article in the field below and click on the submit button.

This tool will use natural language process to determine whether an article is objective or if it includes subjective elements.

A polarity score will be assigned depending on whether a there is a positive, neutral, or negative tone in the article.

Analysis will include a confidence level. This score reflects the confidence in the accuracy of the results.
